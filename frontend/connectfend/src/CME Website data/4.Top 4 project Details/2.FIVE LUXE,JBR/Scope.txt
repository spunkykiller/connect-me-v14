Project Name: FIVE LUXE, JBR
Scope of Work:

Integrated BTU, Hot Water, Cold Water, and Flush Water meters into a centralized IBM AMR software platform.

Enabled automated meter reading (AMR) for all utility meters through a single system.

Solution Delivered:

Implemented seamless data integration from multiple meter types into the IBM software.

Developed customized analytical reports for comprehensive meter data analysis.

Designed and implemented customized billing formats, including all critical billing calculations, as per project requirements.

Business Impact:

Centralized utility meter data management across all services.

Improved billing accuracy and transparency.

Reduced manual effort in data handling and report preparation.

Delivered a scalable AMR solution supporting future expansion.
